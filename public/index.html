This is a demo of maze routing method, based on the 'Lee algorithm'

Stages of the algorithm:

  • Choose a maze size
  • Change the maze

    by clicking on its cells, if you'd like, and press Next.

  • Choose start point

    by clicking on appropriate cell and press Next

  • Choose end point

    by clicking on appropriate cell and press Next

  • Wave expansion

    Click Next to see next Step

  • Backtrace

    Click Next to see next Step

  • Algorithm is finished
